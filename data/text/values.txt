name: name of the difficulty.

mutaspeed: speed the mutant goes when its chasing you.

mutara: max amount the mutant will go away.

sound_weak: value the sound increases within weak sounds

sound_mid: value the sound increases within mid sounds

sound_strong: value the sound increases within strong sounds

customMusic: set it to true if the difficulty has unique custom music.

customSkin: set it to true if the difficulty has a unique skin.

x_frames: max frames the animation has in a custom skin.

stunTimer: amount of stun the mutant has after getting hit by a gun shot.

enableX: enable or disable different items.

addAmmo: amount of ammo the player gets after picking up a gun.
